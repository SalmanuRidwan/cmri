About Us

Citizens’ Mindset Reform Initiative (CMRI) is a non-profit organization committed to rebuilding Nigeria’s moral, civic, and ethical foundations. We work to promote responsible citizenship, restore national values, and inspire behavioral change through strategic programmes, advocacy, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To rebuild Nigeria’s moral, civic, and ethical foundations by restoring national values and shaping a new civic mindset rooted in integrity, discipline, and responsibility.

Our Mision

To design and implement programmes that educate, enlighten, and empower Nigerians to embrace the core values necessary for national development, unity, and prosperity
